The Problem & Our Goal

The Problem: Traditional safety measures are reactive—help is only summoned after an incident occurs.

Our Mission: To transform personal safety with proactive, AI-driven protection.

Shift from Reactive to Proactive

Current apps wait for you to press a button. USafe uses AI to detect danger signs and acts automatically.

95% Threat Detection Accuracy

Using advanced audio processing and ML models, we accurately identify genuine distress signals.

Reduce Response Time

By automating the SOS process, we aim to cut emergency alert times by 40%.

Reduce Human Error

In panic situations, humans freeze. Our automated system removes the burden of action from the victim.
