About USafe

USafe is an intelligent personal safety application designed to protect individuals in real-time using modern technology.

By combining artificial intelligence, location tracking, and community alerts, USafe helps users stay safe in uncertain environments.

Our mission is to create a safer world where technology actively protects people before danger escalates.
